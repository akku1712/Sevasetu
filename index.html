Welcome to Prernapath

At Prernapath, we believe in the power of selfless service to inspire and transform communities. Our journey starts with seva (service), sparking inspiration in individuals, which leads to meaningful change for the betterment of society.

Our Mission

Guided by our tagline, “Seva se Prerna, Prerna se Parivartan”, Prernapath is dedicated to:

  • Encouraging a culture of compassionate service
  • Inspiring positive change in communities
  • Transforming lives through education, outreach, and empowerment

Get Involved

Join the movement—Seva se Prerna, Prerna se Parivartan! Become a part of our mission by volunteering, supporting, or connecting with our initiatives.
